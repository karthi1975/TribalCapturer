## Writing Tips

âœ… **DO**:
- Write like teaching a new MA
- Explain WHY, not just WHAT
- Use numbers for steps: (1), (2), (3)
- Include timing: "within 48 hours", "2-4 weeks later"
- Mention consequences: "or appointment will be rescheduled"

âŒ **DON'T**:
- Use abbreviations without explaining
- Assume knowledge - be explicit
- Skip the "why" - AI learns from it
- Write one-word entries - add context!
